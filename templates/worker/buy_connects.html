Frequently Asked Questions

How many connects do I need to apply for a job?

It depends on the job budget. Budget $20-50 requires 6 connects, $50-150 requires 10 connects, up to 20 connects for $1000+ budgets.

When will my connects be available?

Connects are added to your account immediately after successful payment. You can start applying right away!

Do my connects expire?

No! Your connects never expire. Use them whenever you want—there's no time limit.

Can I get a refund if I don't use all my connects?

Connects are non-refundable, but you can use them anytime. However, if an application is rejected, 3 connects are automatically refunded to your account.

What payment methods do you accept?
