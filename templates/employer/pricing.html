Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What about the 14-day free trial?

New employers get a 14-day trial with Basic features. No credit card required to start.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel with one click. No penalties or hidden fees. Your subscription ends at the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes! Pay annually and get 2 months free (save ~17% off the monthly rate).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, M-Pesa, Pesalink, and bank transfers via Paystack and PesaPal.
